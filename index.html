About Us

Zombies

The Zombies was born out of a shared passion for music in 2023. What began as a hobby, playing covers of our favorite songs, quickly transformed into something more profound. Inspired by the joy of making music together, we decided to take our talents beyond the living room and into the world.

Our journey started with humble beginnings, but soon we found ourselves drawn to the idea of sharing our music with others. From like birthday parties to small events and get-togethers, we sought to bring a touch of acoustic magic to every occasion.

The Zombies consist of seven multi talented individuals. Chanuda Dahamneth on Keyboards, Pawan Bimsara on Lead Guitars, Lakira Dulen on Rhythm Guitars, Chanuth Dulmika on Bass Guitars, Vidura Nipun on Drums and the Vocalists are Dineth Kavikara and Shashini Roshana.
